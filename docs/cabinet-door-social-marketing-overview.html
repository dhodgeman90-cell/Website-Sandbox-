How It Works

  1. Attract — short "watch it get made" videos and finish-comparison posts earn reach organically, no ad spend needed.
  2. Capture — in-app lead forms (not website clicks) pre-fill the buyer's contact info; these convert roughly 2–3× better than sending people to a website.
  3. Nurture — everyone who watched or engaged gets re-targeted, and every new lead gets a fast reply.
  4. Close — the hook is a free, no-obligation quote on the shop's next door order — a low-friction "yes" that starts a real conversation.

Why This Will Yield Results

  • We meet buyers where they already are — nearly half of B2B decision-makers use Facebook for business research. Your buyer is a normal person scrolling in the evening.
  • Low-polish video wins here — in the woodworking/CNC niche, raw, unscripted clips outperform over-produced ads — cheaper to make and better performing.
  • Managed to numbers, not vibes — every dollar is tracked to a cost per lead, and budget moves to what's actually working.
